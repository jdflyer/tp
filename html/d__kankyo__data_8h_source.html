30// startTime and endTime are in the range 0 to 360, which is the same as the current time of day.
31// l_time_attribute and l_time_attribute_boss are arrays of `dKyd_lightSchedjule` with their times
32// set up such that the current time of day will fall in the range of one dKyd_lightSchedjule. For
33// example, if the current time is 110.0f (7:20 AM), then the dKyd_lightSchedjule {105.0f, 135.0f,

35// would use light 2. Since 110.0f is closer to 105.0f than 135.0f, we get a blend of lights 1 and 2
36// which is mostly 1. Blending the lights over time is how we get a smooth day-night transition.
